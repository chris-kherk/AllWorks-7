Common Air Conditioner Problems

  1. Not Cooling: Sometimes, your air conditioner stops cooling well. This could occur because of a dirty filter,low refrigerant, or a broken compressor.
  2. Strange Noises: If your air conditioner makes weird sounds like grinding, squealing, or banging, it could mean parts are loose or there's debris in the system.
  3. Water Leaks: Water pooling around your air conditioner might be due to a blocked drain pipe or a refrigerant leak. This can harm your air conditioner unit and cause water damage to your home.
  4. Bad Smells: If your air conditioner (AC) smells bad, there could be a mold or mildew hiding inside. This doesn't just make the air stinky-it can also make you sick.
  5. Electrical Issues: If your air conditioner (AC) trips the circuit breaker and won't turn on, there might be electrical problems inside the unit or with the wiring.
